About Us

Welcome to Chalukya's Sports Turf, the first-ever multi-sports turf in Mandya! Our mission is to bring the community together through sports and fitness, creating a space where passion meets performance.

Experience the Mandya's First Ever Multi Sports Turf!!

At Chalukya's Sports Turf, we offer world-class facilities for a variety of sports, designed to cater to athletes, enthusiasts, and families alike. Whether you're here to compete, train, or simply have fun, our state-of-the-art turf provides the perfect platform for all your sporting needs.

As pioneers in Mandya, we are dedicated to fostering a vibrant sports culture, promoting health and well-being, and inspiring the next generation of champions. Join us in our journey to make sports accessible, enjoyable, and impactful for everyone.

Facilities:

  • Well maintained turf suitable for cricket and football or any sport of your choice
  • Proper flood lights for evening or night play
  • Spectacular stands or seating zones
  • Clean and well maintained restrooms
  • Ample parking space for visitors
  • Safe place under 24/7 CCTV surveillance
